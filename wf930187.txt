ECONOMY 
Overview: Economic performance is closely tied to the fortunes of the 
oil industry.  Petroleum accounts for more than 90% of export earnings, about 
80% of government revenues, and roughly 40% of GDP. Oman has proved oil 
reserves of 4 billion barrels, equivalent to about 20 years' supply at the 
current rate of extraction. Although agriculture employs a majority of the 
population, urban centers depend on imported food.

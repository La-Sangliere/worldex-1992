GEOGRAPHY

Maritime claims:

Disputes: no defined boundaries with Yemen; location and status of Saudi 
Arabia's boundaries with Qatar and UAE are unresolved; Kuwaiti ownership of 
Qaruh and Umm al Maradim Islands is disputed by Saudi Arabia 
 
Climate: harsh, dry desert with great extremes of temperature

leverage on shipping (especially crude oil) through Persian Gulf and Suez 
Canal

ECONOMY 
Overview: The petroleum sector accounts for roughly 70% of budget 
revenues, 37% of GDP, and almost all export earnings. Saudi Arabia has the 
largest reserves of petroleum in the world, ranks as the largest exporter of 
petroleum, and plays a leading role in OPEC. For the 1990s the government 
intends to encourage private economic activity and to foster the gradual 
process of turning Saudi Arabia into a modern industrial state that retains 
traditional Islamic values.
